## Before adding an image

1. Confirm the licence permits redistribution and modification (CC0, CC BY,
   CC BY-SA, or public domain). **Non-commercial or no-derivatives licences are
   not usable here** — we resize every image.
2. Add a row above, with a link to the source page and the author's name as
   they credit themselves.
3. Prefer CC0 where the choice exists; it costs nothing downstream.
